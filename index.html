Since 1928

NP Tailors was established by Mr. Nanjibhai Popat Rogheliya in the era of Lakhajiraj Jadeja, then the king of Rajkot. His son Mr. Chandrakant Rogheliya took over and made services exclusive to women in 1952. Current owner Mr. Shailesh Rogheliya is the son of Mr. Chandrakant and he is ensuring to continue the legacy of the business.
